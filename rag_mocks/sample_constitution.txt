Test Chama Alpha - Constitution
Article 4: Monthly Contributions
Every member must contribute KES 5,000 virtually every 5th of the month.
Late contributions attract a penalty (fine/bano) of KES 500 per day.
Article 5: Disputes
If a member asks "nikalipa late, fine ni mullah ngapi?", the Chairman (chapo) enforces this KES 500/day fine according to Article 4.
